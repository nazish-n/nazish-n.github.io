Research

My work focuses on designing wireless systems that push sensing and imaging into settings where power, bandwidth, deployment complexity, and environmental constraints make traditional approaches difficult.

Underwater Backscatter

Battery-free and low-power acoustic backscatter systems for underwater communication, sensing, and networking.

mmWave 3D Imaging

Sparse radar sensing and reconstruction methods for object-level 3D perception beyond traditional camera-based pipelines.

Low-Power Sensing

Hardware-software co-design for efficient sensing platforms operating under tight power and deployment constraints.
